👋 Welcome to Costa Blanca Connect
Quick guide to get the most out of your community app
🏠 Getting Started

Costa Blanca Connect is the private app for residents and businesses of Costa Blanca Villas in Farallón, Panamá. Use it to chat with neighbors, share updates, rent a golf cart, support local businesses, and stay informed about what's happening in the community.

Quick orientation

  • Top bar: search box, language toggle (🌐 ES/EN), help (?), notifications (🔔), your avatar
  • Left sidebar: Feed, Marketplace, Events, Safety, Businesses, Neighbors, Messages, Groups, Real Estate, Transport, EMTS
  • Right sidebar: What's Happening, Next Event, Trending Topics, New Neighbors, Emergency Contacts
💡 First time? Click your avatar → How to Use to reopen this guide anytime.
🧭 Where Should I Post? — Sections Explained

Each section in the sidebar has a specific purpose. Posting in the right place keeps the community organized and helps neighbors find what they're looking for.

📰 Feed — General community chatter

The main Feed is for everyday updates, questions, polls, photos, lost & found, and conversations with neighbors. Think of it as the village square. If your post doesn't fit a more specific section below, it belongs here.

🛒 Marketplace — Buy, sell, give away

Use Marketplace for any physical item you want to sell, give away free, or list with a price. Includes condition, category, and photos. Don't post items for sale in the Feed — they belong here so buyers can browse.

📅 Events — Scheduled happenings with a date & time

Events are anything with a specific date — beach cleanup, BBQ, HOA meeting, yoga class, birthday party. Neighbors can RSVP (going / maybe / can't go). Don't announce events in the Feed — create them here so they show up on the calendar and in "Next Event."

🚨 Safety — Alerts & security concerns

Safety is for sharing security incidents, suspicious activity, or hazards with neighbors (e.g. break-in attempts, stray dogs, road damage). Reports here do NOT reach police, fire, or medical services — for true emergencies, call emergency officials directly.

🏪 Businesses — Local services directory

Businesses is the directory of restaurants, shops, and services. Browse, leave reviews, mark favorites. Business owners can claim their listing to manage it. Don't post business promotions in the Feed — claim your business page instead.

👥 Groups — Smaller circles within the community

Groups are sub-communities for shared interests (book club, fitness, pets, etc.). Posts inside a group only show to its members. Use Groups when a topic is relevant to some neighbors but not all of them.

🏡 Real Estate — Properties for sale or rent

Listings managed by Uncover Panama Real Estate. Don't post property listings in the Feed — direct any rental/sale inquiries here.

🚗 Transport & 🛺 Golf Carts

Getting around — taxis to Panama City, airport transfers, and golf cart rentals within Costa Blanca Villas.

🆘 EMTS — Emergency Medical info

Info about EMTS Panama (private EMS), member pricing, and the closest emergency contacts. Always-visible in the right sidebar too.

💡 Quick rule of thumb: if it has a price → Marketplace. If it has a date → Events. If it's a safety concern → Safety. Anything else → Feed.
👤 Your Profile & Settings

Edit your profile

  1. Click your avatar in the top-right → My Profile
  2. Click Edit Profile to change your display name, bio, or address
  3. Click your profile picture to upload a new one (cropped to a circle)
  4. Click your banner to upload a cover photo for your profile

Settings

Click your avatar → Settings to see:

  • Profile — view your username and email (read-only), change your password, edit name and address
  • Notifications — toggle which alerts you receive (new posts, safety, events, comments, group activity, marketplace, etc.)

Get verified

  1. If you don't have the ✓ Verified badge, go to your profile
  2. Tap Get Verified
  3. Upload a document showing your name + address (utility bill, lease, official mail)
  4. An admin reviews within 1–2 days. You'll be notified when verified.
💡 Verified neighbors get a blue checkmark next to their name everywhere — builds trust with others.
📝 Posts, Reactions & Comments

Create a post

  1. Go to Feed
  2. Click the "What's happening in Costa Blanca Villas?" box
  3. Type your post. Optionally:
    • 📷 Attach a photo
    • 📊 Add a poll (question + 2 or more options)
    • 📍 Add a location tag
  4. Click + Post to publish

React to a post

  1. Hover (or long-press on mobile) the React button under any post
  2. Pick from 👍 Like, 💡 Insightful, 😂 Haha, 😮 Wow, 😢 Sad, 👏 Agree
  3. Click your reaction again to remove it

The post author gets a notification when you react.

Comment on a post

  1. Click Comment under a post
  2. Type your reply, hit Enter or click Send
  3. The post author gets a notification
  4. You can delete your own comment with the trash icon

Vote on polls

Just tap the option you want. You can change your vote anytime by tapping a different one. Tap your current option again to remove your vote.

Edit or delete your own post

Click the ⋯ menu in the top-right of your post → Edit or Delete.

📤 Sharing Posts

Click Share at the bottom of any post. A modal opens with 5 options:

  • 📰 Share to Community Feed — reposts to your own feed with a preview card. Add an optional note.
  • 💬 WhatsApp — opens WhatsApp with the post link pre-filled. Pick a contact or group.
  • ✉️ Email — opens your email app with subject and link ready
  • 📋 Copy Link — paste anywhere
  • 📲 More Apps — only on phones; opens the device's native share sheet
💡 When you "Share to Community Feed", the original post shows up as an embedded card inside your post. Click the embed to jump back to the original.
👥 Groups

Find & join groups

  1. Click Groups in the left sidebar
  2. Use the search bar to find by name or description
  3. Use the category filter chips (🍷 Social, ⛳ Sports & Fitness, 🏖️ Beach & Outdoors, 🐕 Pets, etc.) to browse
  4. On any group card, click Join Group (public) or 🔒 Request to Join (private)

Create a group

  1. Click + Create Group at the top of the Groups page
  2. Fill in Group Name, pick a Category, write a Description
  3. Optionally upload a Group Photo (square icon) and Cover Photo (wide banner)
  4. Pick Privacy: 🌐 Public (anyone joins) or 🔒 Private (you approve members)
  5. Click Create Group. You become the creator/admin automatically.

Post inside a group

On the group page, use the composer at the top to share with members:

  • 📷 Photo · 📄 PDF · 📊 Poll · 📅 Event
  • Click 📅 Event to schedule a group event with date, time, and location — RSVPable, members get notified
  • Hit Post when ready. All members get a notification.

Add a co-admin (creator only)

Co-admins can manage posts, members, and group settings — but can't delete the group. Useful as a backup.

  1. Open the group page
  2. In the Members card, click "See all X members ▾" to expand
  3. Find the member you want to promote
  4. Click "Make Co-Admin" (blue outline button) next to their name
  5. They'll get a notification: "You're now a co-admin of [Group]" and a ⭐ CO-ADMIN badge

To remove a co-admin: same place, click "Remove Co-Admin".

💡 Designate at least one co-admin for every active group — if you ever step away, they keep the group running.

Edit, invite, kick, leave

  • Edit Group (creator/co-admin) — top of group page; change name, description, category, privacy, photos
  • + Invite Neighbor — opens a list of all neighbors; click Invite next to anyone
  • Remove member (creator/co-admin) — in expanded member list, click red Remove next to their name
  • Leave group — on the group card or page, click Leave
  • Delete group (creator only) — top of group page, red 🗑️ Delete button
🛒 Marketplace

Browse & buy

  1. Click Marketplace in the sidebar
  2. Use the category chips at the top to filter
  3. Click any item to see details and contact the seller

List an item

  1. Click + List Item
  2. Add a Title, Price (or check Free), Category, Condition, Description, and a Photo
  3. Click Post Listing

All neighbors get a notification: "Steve listed 'Surfboard' in the Marketplace for $200"

Manage your listings

  • ✏️ Edit — update price, description, photo
  • ✓ Mark as Sold — keeps the listing visible but greyed out
  • 🗑️ Delete — removes it entirely
⚠️ Marketplace is for local items only — no Panama City, no other provinces, no out-of-country listings. (Member Agreement Section 5.)
📅 Events

RSVP to an event

  1. Go to Events in the sidebar
  2. Click an event to see details
  3. Click ✓ Going, ? Maybe, or ✕ Can't Go
  4. The event host gets a notification

Create an event

  1. On the Events page, click + Create Event
  2. Fill in Title, Date, Start time, End time, Location, Description, Category
  3. Optionally upload a photo
  4. Click Create Event

Anyone can RSVP. As the host, you get a notification each time someone does.

Group events

You can also schedule events from inside a group — see the Groups section. Group events appear in both the group feed and the main Events page (tagged with the group name).

🛺 Golf Cart Rentals

Find a cart

  1. Click Transport in the sidebar
  2. Browse listed carts under Community Cart Rentals
  3. Click the green 💬 WhatsApp button on any listing — opens a chat with the owner with a friendly intro pre-filled

List your own cart

  1. Click + List My Cart
  2. Add a Photo of the cart
  3. Fill in Description (e.g. "4-seater, 2023, good condition")
  4. Set a Rate (e.g. $25/day)
  5. Add your WhatsApp number with country code — +507 6790-4807 for Panama, +1 555-123-4567 for USA/Canada
  6. Optional Notes (e.g. "Weekends only")
  7. Click Post Listing

All neighbors get notified about your new listing.

Manage your listing

On your own listings you'll see:

  • ✏️ Edit (blue) — change description, rate, contact, photo
  • 🗑 Remove (red) — take it down
⚠️ Always include the country code in your WhatsApp number, or the chat link won't work.
💬 Direct Messages

Start a conversation

  1. Click any neighbor's name or avatar anywhere in the app to view their profile
  2. Click the Message button on their profile
  3. Type your message and send

Or open Messages in the sidebar to see all your existing conversations.

Get notified

You'll see an unread badge on the bell icon and on Messages in the sidebar when someone messages you. Open the message to mark it read.

Block a user

  1. Open their profile (or open the conversation and click their name at the top)
  2. Click Block
  3. They can no longer message you. Unblock anytime from the same spot.
💡 Messages are 1-on-1 only. For group chats, create a Group instead.
🏪 Businesses & Reviews

Find a local business

  1. Click Businesses in the sidebar
  2. Filter by category, search by name
  3. Click any business to see details, hours, photos, reviews, and contact options

Recommend a business

  1. Open the business page
  2. Click + Recommend or Leave a Review
  3. Pick a star rating (1–5), write your review, optionally add a photo
  4. Click Post. The business owner is notified.

Contact a business

  • Tap 📞 Call to dial
  • Tap 💬 WhatsApp if listed
  • Tap 📧 Email if listed
🏠 Real Estate

The Real Estate section shows properties for sale and rent in and around Costa Blanca Villas. Listings are exclusively managed by Uncover Panama Real Estate, our official partner.

  • Use the For Sale / For Rent tabs to filter
  • Click any listing to see details and contact info
  • Tap View All Listings → on the sponsor banner to visit Uncover Panama's site
💡 Have a property to list? Contact Uncover Panama directly via the link on the Real Estate page.
🚨 Safety, EMTS & Important Links

Safety section

Use the Safety tab in the sidebar to:

  • Post lost & found items, suspicious activity, road conditions
  • See alerts from neighbors
  • Important community announcements appear pinned at the top

EMTS page

Click EMTS in the sidebar — your one-stop emergency hub:

  • 🚑 Services — EMTS Panama (private EMS) info and member pricing
  • 📞 Emergency Phrases — Spanish phrases for medical, fire, police
  • ⚡ Be Prepared — checklists for first aid, vehicle kit, generators, AEDs
  • 🔗 Important Links — Naturgy (power), IMHPA (weather), Mas Móvil, Tigo, plus Panama Emergency Guide PDF and donate to IERF
  • 👤 My Info — personal medical info form for first responders (saved on your device)

Emergency contacts (always visible)

The right sidebar always shows emergency numbers — SUME, EMTS, police, fire. Tap to call directly.

⚠️ For real emergencies, always call 911 or the appropriate authority directly. Don't rely on this app as your primary channel.

Important: Some local Panama numbers (3-digit short codes, *codes, and certain landlines) only work from a Panamanian phone line — they may not connect from foreign mobile carriers, roaming SIMs, or apps like WhatsApp/FaceTime. If you live here, get a Panama SIM. Visitors should save the full international format (e.g. +507 6790-4807) and use that.
🔔 Notifications

The 🔔 bell icon shows a red badge when you have unread activity. Click it to see your notifications. You'll be notified when:

  • Someone comments or reacts to your post
  • Someone messages you
  • Someone RSVPs to your event
  • A neighbor posts a new marketplace item or cart rental
  • A new event is scheduled in a group you belong to
  • Someone posts in a group you belong to
  • You're promoted to co-admin of a group
  • (Business) Someone leaves a review on your business or you receive a reply to your review

Open the notifications page to mark them all read. You can turn individual notification types on/off in Settings.

🌐 Language & Accessibility

Click 🌐 ES in the top bar to translate the entire app to Spanish (powered by Google Translate). Click again to return to English.

Your language choice is saved on your device — next time you log in, the app remembers.

💡 The login page has a separate ENG / ESP toggle at the bottom.
