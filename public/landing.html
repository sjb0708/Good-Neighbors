How It Works
Simple, private,
built for your community

Platform Features
Everything your
neighborhood needs
From safety alerts to the local marketplace, Costa Blanca Connect keeps the whole community connected in one private, verified space.
📰

Community Feed

A real-time feed of posts from your verified neighbors — news, recommendations, questions, and general community conversation. Organized, relevant, and noise-free.

🚨

Safety & Security Alerts

Instantly share and receive verified security alerts, suspicious activity reports, and important safety notices from neighbors and HOA — not from strangers on the internet.

🛒

Neighborhood Marketplace

Buy, sell, give away, or find items within the community. From patio furniture to golf carts, if it's happening in Costa Blanca, it's on the marketplace.

🎉

Events & Activities

Stay up to date on community events, beach cleanups, HOA meetings, and local gatherings. RSVP, invite neighbors, and never miss what's happening.

🏢

Business Directory

A curated directory of verified local businesses — restaurants, contractors, realtors, and services — recommended and reviewed by your actual neighbors.

🏠

HOA Communications

Official announcements, governance updates, and meeting minutes from the Costa Blanca Villas HOA — all in one place, directly in your feed.
